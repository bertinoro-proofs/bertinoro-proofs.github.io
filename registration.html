PROOFS@BiCi: Efficient Probabilistic Proofs

Registration

Participation in this workshop is by invitation only.

A registration form for invited participants will be available on this page.

The all-inclusive registration fee is €665 for accomodation in a single room and €590 for accomodation in a double room. These fees cover all local expenses, including meals, coffee breaks, and accomodation for 5 nights (from arrival Sunday afternoon through departure on Friday afternoon).

Accommodation is provided on the wonderful premises of Bertinoro Castle.

A limited number of fellowships are available for participants with difficult financial conditions, via the BiCi Leonardo Melandri Program. Interested participants can send an inquiry to info@bici.eu.
